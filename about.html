JapanBot!

JapanBot is a chatbot designed to help you learn to read Japanese!

JapanBot is meant to teach Japanese language beginners the basics of hiragana and katakana, and allow them to read a significant amount of Japanese, and write any Japanese word that they learn in a way which can be understood by every Japanese speaker.

Thanks to Steve, Noah, Sean, and Josh for working on JapanBot during HackNYU 2018! :)
